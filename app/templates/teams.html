Base Camp Coding Academy Leadership Project Teams

Management Team

The management team oversees the chores done at Base Camp and ensures everyone is on task and on time.

Procurement Team

The procurement team orders all supplies needed at Base Camp and sets out and cleans up lunch.

Documentation Team

The documentation team photographs all events, follows up with guest speakers, and manages Base Camp's social medias.

Community Team

The community team plans all community events for students, as well as hosts parties for birthdays and holidays.
